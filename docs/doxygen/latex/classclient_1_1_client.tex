Creates and configures the player entity in the ECS. 

Constructs a player entity and attaches its initial components\+: position, velocity, movement speed, render asset, sprite source rectangle, scale, player tag, and sprite animation metadata.

The created entity is positioned at (100, 100) with zero initial velocity and uses \doxylink{namespacerender_manager_abf8018f3bfedadd2f7c4e1ebf79385da}{render\+Manager\+::\+PLAYER\+\_\+\+PATH} for rendering. Sprite and scale values are taken from \doxylink{structclient_1_1_player_sprite_config}{Player\+Sprite\+Config}.
